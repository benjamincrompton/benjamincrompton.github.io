Simulation Examples

Rigid multi-body model derived from DICOMs

Developed a rigid multi-body model, complete with articular cartilage, pre-loaded ligaments, and tendons to evaluate the wrist in different orientations. A stage 4 scapholunate dissociation was simulated, and the model was used to explore different tunnel/anchor positions. From there, optimal positions could be evaluated for the surgeon.

FEA: ISO 7206-6 (Neck Fatigue) & ISO 7206-4 (Stem Fatigue)

Early design verification of a femoral stem implant.

CFD: Parametric Study to Optimise Cannula design for Heart Pump

Developed a model of an aorta and left ventricular assist device (LVAD). The study focussed on altering the geometry of the cannula of the LVAD to optimise the blood flow in the aorta. Optimisation was done by minimising the shear forces on the aortic walls, which in turn would reduce the risk of stroke post-LVAD implantation.
